Analitik (Analytics) ve Veri Gorsellestirme (Data Visualization) - Pratik Ornekler

Uygulamali alistirmalar ile pencere fonksiyonlari (window functions), ileri toplamalar (advanced aggregations), CTE'ler (Common Table Expressions) ve gorsellestirme (visualization) icin veri hazirlama konularinda uzmanlasin.

Ogrenme Hedefleri:
  • Siralama (ranking), kumulatif toplamlar (running totals) ve karsilastirmalar icin pencere fonksiyonlarini (window functions) uygulayin
  • Ileri toplamalari (advanced aggregations) kullanin (GROUPING SETS, ROLLUP, CUBE)
  • CTE'ler (Common Table Expressions) ile karmasik analitik olusturun
  • Gorsellestirme (visualization) araclari icin veri hazirlayin

Bolum 1: Pencere Fonksiyonlari (Window Functions)

Siralama, toplama ve satirlar arasi karsilastirma pratigi yapin.

üí° Pratik Ipucu: Pencere fonksiyonlari, SQL'in en guclu analitik araclaridir. Her alistirmada OVER() yan tumcesine dikkat edin - PARTITION BY verileri gruplar, ORDER BY ise sirayi belirler. GROUP BY'dan farkli olarak, pencere fonksiyonlari tum satirlari korur.

Alistirma 1.1: Siralama Fonksiyonlari (Ranking Functions) Karsilastirmasi

Aylik satis verileri verildiginde, ROW_NUMBER(), RANK() ve DENSE_RANK() arasindaki farki gosterin:

month sales
2024-01 50000
2024-02 45000
2024-03 45000
2024-04 42000
2024-05 55000

Gorev: Her uc siralama fonksiyonunu da sales DESC'e gore siralayan bir sorgu yazin. 

WITH monthly_sales AS (
    SELECT '2024-01' AS month, 50000 AS sales UNION ALL
    SELECT '2024-02', 45000 UNION ALL
    SELECT '2024-03', 45000 UNION ALL
    SELECT '2024-04', 42000 UNION ALL
    SELECT '2024-05', 55000
)
SELECT
    month,
    sales,
    ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY sales DESC) AS row_num,
    RANK() OVER (ORDER BY sales DESC) AS rank,
    DENSE_RANK() OVER (ORDER BY sales DESC) AS dense_rank
FROM monthly_sales;

Sonuc:

month sales row_num rank dense_rank
2024-05 55000 1 1 1
2024-01 50000 2 2 2
2024-02 45000 3 3 3
2024-03 45000 4 3 3
2024-04 42000 5 5 4
Temel Farklar:
  • ROW_NUMBER(): Her zaman benzersiz (esitler icin 3, 4)
  • RANK(): Esitler icin ayni siralama, sonrakini atlar (3, 3, 5)
  • DENSE_RANK(): Esitler icin ayni siralama, atlama yok (3, 3, 4)

Alistirma 1.2: Grup Basina En Iyi N (Top N Per Group)

Her kategorideki en cok satan 3 urunu bulun.

Gorev: ROW_NUMBER() ile PARTITION BY kullanarak urunleri her kategori icinde siralayin, sonra en iyi 3'e filtreleyin.

WITH product_sales AS (
    SELECT
        p.id AS product_id,
        p.name AS product_name,
        c.name AS category_name,
        SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) AS total_sales
    FROM products p
    JOIN categories c ON p.category_id = c.id
    JOIN order_lines ol ON p.id = ol.product_id
    GROUP BY p.id, p.name, c.name
),
ranked_products AS (
    SELECT
        product_name,
        category_name,
        total_sales,
        ROW_NUMBER() OVER (
            PARTITION BY category_name
            ORDER BY total_sales DESC
        ) AS rank_in_category
    FROM product_sales
)
SELECT
    category_name,
    rank_in_category,
    product_name,
    total_sales
FROM ranked_products
WHERE rank_in_category <= 3
ORDER BY category_name, rank_in_category;
Kullanim Alani: Bu kalip asagidaki gibi "En Iyi N" raporlari icin onemlidir:
  • Bolge basina en iyi 5 musteri
  • Ay basina en iyi 10 urun
  • Ceyrek basina en iyi 3 satis temsilcisi

Alistirma 1.3: Trend Analizi (Trend Analysis) icin LAG ve LEAD

LAG() kullanarak aydan aya satis buyumesini hesaplayin.

Gorev: Her ayin satislarini, onceki ayin satislarini ve yuzde degisimi gosterin.

WITH monthly_sales AS (
    SELECT
        DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) AS month,
        SUM(total_amount) AS sales
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date)
)
SELECT
    month,
    sales,
    LAG(sales) OVER (ORDER BY month) AS prev_month_sales,
    sales - LAG(sales) OVER (ORDER BY month) AS absolute_change,
    ROUND(
        (sales - LAG(sales) OVER (ORDER BY month)) * 100.0 /
        NULLIF(LAG(sales) OVER (ORDER BY month), 0),
        2
    ) AS pct_change
FROM monthly_sales
ORDER BY month;

Ornek Cikti:

month sales prev_month change pct_change
2024-01-01 50000 NULL NULL NULL
2024-02-01 55000 50000 5000 10.00
2024-03-01 48000 55000 -7000 -12.73
LAG ve LEAD Karsilastirmasi:
  • LAG(col, n) - n satir ONCESINDEN deger alir (varsayilan n=1)
  • LEAD(col, n) - n satir SONRASINDAN deger alir

Alistirma 1.4: Kumulatif Toplamlar (Running Totals) ve Hareketli Ortalamalar (Moving Averages)

Kumulatif satis ve 7 gunluk hareketli ortalama hesaplayin.

Gorev: Kumulatif toplam ve hareketli ortalama hesaplamak icin pencere cerceve ifadelerini kullanin.

SELECT
    order_date,
    daily_total AS daily_sales,
    -- Kumulatif toplam: baslangictan mevcut satira kadar tum satirlar
    SUM(daily_total) OVER (
        ORDER BY order_date
        ROWS BETWEEN UNBOUNDED PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW
    ) AS running_total,
    -- 7 gunluk hareketli ortalama: onceki 6 + mevcut
    ROUND(
        AVG(daily_total) OVER (
            ORDER BY order_date
            ROWS BETWEEN 6 PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW
        ),
        2
    ) AS moving_avg_7day,
    -- Hareketli penceredeki satir sayisi
    COUNT(*) OVER (
        ORDER BY order_date
        ROWS BETWEEN 6 PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW
    ) AS days_in_window
FROM (
    SELECT order_date, SUM(total_amount) AS daily_total
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY order_date
) daily_totals
ORDER BY order_date;
Cerceve Ifadesi Tuzagi:
  • ROWS fiziksel satirlari sayar
  • RANGE deger araliklerini dikkate alir (esitleri farkli sekilde ele alir)
  • ORDER BY olmadan, cerceve tum bolumeyi icerir

Bolum 2: Ileri Toplamalar (Advanced Aggregations)

GROUPING SETS, ROLLUP, CUBE ve kosullu toplamalarda uzmanlasin.

üí° Pratik Ipucu: GROUPING SETS, ROLLUP ve CUBE, Excel'in pivot tablolarina benzer sonuclar uretir. GROUPING() fonksiyonu, hangi satirlarin alt toplam oldugunu belirlemenize yardimci olur (1 = toplam satiri, 0 = detay satiri).

Alistirma 2.1: Coklu Ozetler (Multiple Summaries) icin GROUPING SETS

Asagidakilere gore satislari gosteren bir rapor olusturun:

  • Kategori + Ay
  • Sadece Kategori
  • Sadece Ay
  • Genel toplam

Gorev: Tum ozetleri tek bir sorguda uretmek icin GROUPING SETS kullanin.

SELECT
    COALESCE(c.name, 'Tum Kategoriler') AS category,
    COALESCE(TO_CHAR(o.order_date, 'YYYY-MM'), 'Tum Aylar') AS month,
    SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) AS total_sales,
    COUNT(DISTINCT o.id) AS order_count,
    -- Gruplama seviyesini belirle
    GROUPING(c.name) AS is_category_total,
    GROUPING(TO_CHAR(o.order_date, 'YYYY-MM')) AS is_month_total
FROM orders o
JOIN order_lines ol ON o.id = ol.order_id
JOIN products p ON ol.product_id = p.id
JOIN categories c ON p.category_id = c.id
GROUP BY GROUPING SETS (
    (c.name, TO_CHAR(o.order_date, 'YYYY-MM')),  -- Detay
    (c.name),                                      -- Kategori alt toplami
    (TO_CHAR(o.order_date, 'YYYY-MM')),            -- Ay alt toplami
    ()                                             -- Genel toplam
)
ORDER BY
    GROUPING(c.name),
    GROUPING(TO_CHAR(o.order_date, 'YYYY-MM')),
    category,
    month;
GROUPING() Fonksiyonu: Sutun toplanmissa (gruplama nedeniyle NULL) 1, gercek bir degerse 0 dondurur. NULL degerlerini toplama NULL'larindan ayirt etmek icin kullanislidir.

Alistirma 2.2: Hiyerarsik Toplamlar (Hierarchical Totals) icin ROLLUP

Hiyerarsik bir satis raporu olusturun: Yil > Ceyrek > Ay her seviyede alt toplamlarla.

Gorev: Alt toplamlari otomatik olusturmak icin ROLLUP kullanin.

SELECT
    EXTRACT(YEAR FROM order_date) AS year,
    EXTRACT(QUARTER FROM order_date) AS quarter,
    EXTRACT(MONTH FROM order_date) AS month,
    SUM(total_amount) AS total_sales,
    COUNT(*) AS order_count,
    CASE
        WHEN GROUPING(EXTRACT(YEAR FROM order_date)) = 1 THEN 'Genel Toplam'
        WHEN GROUPING(EXTRACT(QUARTER FROM order_date)) = 1 THEN 'Yil Toplami'
        WHEN GROUPING(EXTRACT(MONTH FROM order_date)) = 1 THEN 'Ceyrek Toplami'
        ELSE 'Ay Detayi'
    END AS level_type
FROM orders
GROUP BY ROLLUP (
    EXTRACT(YEAR FROM order_date),
    EXTRACT(QUARTER FROM order_date),
    EXTRACT(MONTH FROM order_date)
)
ORDER BY year NULLS LAST, quarter NULLS LAST, month NULLS LAST;

ROLLUP su satirlari uretir:

  • (year, quarter, month) - Detay satirlari
  • (year, quarter) - Ceyrek alt toplamlari
  • (year) - Yillik alt toplamlar
  • () - Genel toplam

Alistirma 2.3: FILTER ile Kosullu Toplama (Conditional Aggregation)

Her ay icin toplam siparisler, teslim edilen siparisler ve iptal edilen siparisleri gosteren bir rapor olusturun.

Gorev: Kosullu sayim icin FILTER ifadesini kullanin.

SELECT
    DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) AS month,
    COUNT(*) AS total_orders,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'delivered') AS delivered,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'cancelled') AS cancelled,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'processing') AS processing,
    ROUND(
        COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'delivered') * 100.0 / COUNT(*),
        2
    ) AS delivery_rate,
    SUM(total_amount) FILTER (WHERE status = 'delivered') AS delivered_revenue
FROM orders
GROUP BY DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date)
ORDER BY month;
Alternatif (FILTER olmadan): 
SUM(CASE WHEN status = 'delivered' THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS delivered
FILTER daha temizdir ve PostgreSQL'e ozgudur.

Alistirma 2.4: Istatistiksel Toplamalar (Statistical Aggregations)

Her kategori icinde urun fiyatlari icin istatistiksel metrikler hesaplayin.

Gorev: Ortalama, medyan, standart sapma hesaplayin ve aykiri degerleri belirleyin.

SELECT
    c.name AS category_name,
    COUNT(*) AS product_count,
    ROUND(AVG(p.price), 2) AS avg_price,
    ROUND(
        PERCENTILE_CONT(0.5) WITHIN GROUP (ORDER BY p.price),
        2
    ) AS median_price,
    ROUND(STDDEV(p.price), 2) AS stddev_price,
    MIN(p.price) AS min_price,
    MAX(p.price) AS max_price,
    ROUND(
        PERCENTILE_CONT(0.25) WITHIN GROUP (ORDER BY p.price),
        2
    ) AS q1,
    ROUND(
        PERCENTILE_CONT(0.75) WITHIN GROUP (ORDER BY p.price),
        2
    ) AS q3
FROM products p
JOIN categories c ON p.category_id = c.id
GROUP BY c.name
ORDER BY avg_price DESC;

Aykiri Degerleri Bulma (Z-Skoru > 2):

WITH category_stats AS (
    SELECT
        p.id AS product_id,
        p.name,
        p.price,
        c.name AS category_name,
        AVG(p.price) OVER (PARTITION BY c.id) AS avg_price,
        STDDEV(p.price) OVER (PARTITION BY c.id) AS stddev_price
    FROM products p
    JOIN categories c ON p.category_id = c.id
)
SELECT
    name,
    category_name,
    price,
    ROUND((price - avg_price) / NULLIF(stddev_price, 0), 2) AS z_score
FROM category_stats
WHERE ABS((price - avg_price) / NULLIF(stddev_price, 0)) > 2;

Bolum 3: Karmasik Analitik (Analytics) icin CTE'ler (Common Table Expressions)

Common Table Expression'lar ile cok adimli analitik sorgular olusturun.

üí° Pratik Ipucu: CTE'leri "sorgu icindeki gecici tablolar" gibi dusunun. WITH kelimesiyle baslarlar ve karmasik analizleri adim adim yapmanizi saglarlar. Her CTE bir oncekini kullanabilir, bu sayede karmasik hesaplamalari parcalara bolerek yazabilirsiniz.

Alistirma 3.1: Kohort Tutma Analizi (Cohort Retention Analysis)

Ilk satin alma aylarina gore musteri tutma oranini gosteren bir kohort analizi olusturun.

Kohort Analizi Nedir?
Kohort, ortak bir ozellige sahip musteri grubudur - bu ornekte, ilk satin alma ayi. Kohort tutma analizi, her gruptan kac musterinin sonraki aylarda geri gelip alisveris yaptigini takip eder.

Gorev: Her kohorttan musterilerin yuzde kacinin sonraki aylarda alisveris yaptigini hesaplayin.

Adim Adim Yaklasim: Bu karmasik analiz 4 CTE'den olusur. Tam cozumu gormeden once her adimi anlayalim.
Adim 1: Musteri Kohortlarini Belirle

Soru: Her musterinin "kohort ayi" (ilk satin alma ayi) nasil bulunur?

-- Her musterinin ilk satin alma ayini bul (kohortlari)
SELECT
    customer_id,
    DATE_TRUNC('month', MIN(order_date)) AS cohort_month
FROM orders
GROUP BY customer_id;

Aciklama: MIN(order_date) ilk siparis tarihini bulur, DATE_TRUNC('month', ...) bunu ayin ilk gunune yuvarlar.

Adim 2: Kohorttan Bu Yana Gecen Aylari Hesapla

Soru: Her siparis icin, musterinin kohort ayindan bu yana kac ay gectigini nasil hesaplariz?

-- Kohort ile her aktivite arasindaki aylari hesapla
(EXTRACT(YEAR FROM activity_month) - EXTRACT(YEAR FROM cohort_month)) * 12 +
(EXTRACT(MONTH FROM activity_month) - EXTRACT(MONTH FROM cohort_month)) AS months_since_cohort

Aciklama: Toplam ay farkini hesaplamak icin yillari ve aylari ayri ayri cikariyoruz. Ay 0 = ilk satin alma ayi, Ay 1 = sonraki ay, vb.

Adim 3: Kohort Buyukluklerini Say

Soru: Her kohortta kac musteri oldugunu nasil sayariz?

-- Her kohorttaki benzersiz musteri sayisi
SELECT
    cohort_month,
    COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) AS cohort_customers
FROM customer_cohort
GROUP BY cohort_month;

Aciklama: Bu bize her kohort icin "%100" temel cizgisini verir.

Adim 4: Tutma Oranini Hesapla

Soru: Geri donen musterilerin yuzdesini nasil hesaplariz?

-- Tutma yuzdesi formulu
ROUND(active_customers * 100.0 / cohort_customers, 1) AS retention_pct

Aciklama: Aktif musterileri toplam kohort musterilerine bol, yuzde icin 100 ile carp.

Tam Cozum
WITH
-- Adim 1: Her musterinin kohortunu belirle (ilk satin alma ayi)
customer_cohort AS (
    SELECT
        customer_id,
        DATE_TRUNC('month', MIN(order_date)) AS cohort_month
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY customer_id
),
-- Adim 2: Her siparis icin kohorttan bu yana gecen aylari hesapla
customer_activity AS (
    SELECT
        cc.customer_id,
        cc.cohort_month,
        DATE_TRUNC('month', o.order_date) AS activity_month,
        (EXTRACT(YEAR FROM DATE_TRUNC('month', o.order_date)) -
         EXTRACT(YEAR FROM cc.cohort_month)) * 12 +
        (EXTRACT(MONTH FROM DATE_TRUNC('month', o.order_date)) -
         EXTRACT(MONTH FROM cc.cohort_month)) AS months_since_cohort
    FROM customer_cohort cc
    JOIN orders o ON cc.customer_id = o.customer_id
),
-- Adim 3: Kohort buyuklugunu say
cohort_size AS (
    SELECT
        cohort_month,
        COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) AS cohort_customers
    FROM customer_cohort
    GROUP BY cohort_month
),
-- Adim 4: Her kohort ve donem icin aktif musterileri say
retention_data AS (
    SELECT
        cohort_month,
        months_since_cohort,
        COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) AS active_customers
    FROM customer_activity
    GROUP BY cohort_month, months_since_cohort
)
-- Son: Tutma oranini hesapla
SELECT
    r.cohort_month,
    cs.cohort_customers,
    r.months_since_cohort,
    r.active_customers,
    ROUND(r.active_customers * 100.0 / cs.cohort_customers, 1) AS retention_pct
FROM retention_data r
JOIN cohort_size cs ON r.cohort_month = cs.cohort_month
WHERE r.months_since_cohort <= 12
ORDER BY r.cohort_month, r.months_since_cohort;

Alistirma 3.2: Musteri Yasam Boyu Degeri (Customer Lifetime Value - CLV)

Musteri yasam boyu degeri metriklerini hesaplayin ve musterileri segmentleyin.

Musteri Yasam Boyu Degeri Nedir?
CLV, bir isletmenin tek bir musteriden tum iliski boyunca bekleyebilecegi toplam geliri tahmin eder. Hangi musterilerin en degerli oldugunu ve nasil segmentlenecegini belirlemeye yardimci olur.

Gorev: Toplam harcama, siklik, yakinlik hesaplayin ve gelecek degeri tahmin edin.

Adim Adim Yaklasim: Bunu 3 parcada olusturacagiz: temel metrikleri hesapla, kartil siralama ekle, sonra musterileri segmentle.
Adim 1: Temel Musteri Metriklerini Hesapla

Soru: Her musteri icin hangi metrikleri hesaplamamiz gerekiyor? (Ipucu: harcama, siklik ve yakinlik dusunun)

SELECT
    customer_id,
    COUNT(*) AS total_orders,           -- Siklik: Ne siklikta aliyorlar
    SUM(total_amount) AS total_spent,   -- Parasal: Ne kadar harciyorlar
    AVG(total_amount) AS avg_order_value,
    MIN(order_date) AS first_order,     -- Ne zaman basladilar
    MAX(order_date) AS last_order,      -- En son siparis
    CURRENT_DATE - MAX(order_date) AS days_since_last  -- Yakinlik
FROM orders
GROUP BY customer_id;

Aciklama: Bunlar musteri analizinde yaygin olarak kullanilan RFM (Recency/Yakinlik, Frequency/Siklik, Monetary/Parasal) metrikleridir.

Adim 2: Kartil Siralama icin NTILE'i Anla

Soru: NTILE(4) ne yapar ve segmentasyon icin neden faydalidir?

-- NTILE(4) musterileri 4 esit gruba (kartil) boler
NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY total_spent DESC) AS spending_quartile
-- Kartil 1 = En cok harcayan %25
-- Kartil 4 = En az harcayan %25

Aciklama: NTILE satirlari N esit gruba boler. Kartil 1 = en iyi performans, Kartil 4 = dikkat gerektiren.

Adim 3: Musteri Segmentleri Olustur

Soru: Kartil siralamalarini "Sampiyonlar" veya "Risk Altinda" gibi anlamli musteri segmentleri olusturmak icin nasil birlestirebiliriz?

-- Kartil kombinasyonlarina gore segmentle
CASE
    WHEN spending_quartile = 1 AND frequency_quartile = 1 THEN 'Sampiyonlar'
    WHEN spending_quartile <= 2 AND recency_quartile = 1 THEN 'Sadik'
    WHEN recency_quartile >= 3 AND spending_quartile <= 2 THEN 'Risk Altinda'
    WHEN recency_quartile = 4 THEN 'Kaybedilmis'
    ELSE 'Normal'
END AS segment

Aciklama:

  • Sampiyonlar: Sik sik alim yapan yuksek harcayicilar
  • Sadik: Yakin zamanda alisveris yapan iyi harcayicilar
  • Risk Altinda: Eskiden iyi harcardi ama yakin zamanda alim yapmadi
  • Kaybedilmis: Uzun suredir alisveris yapmamis
Tam Cozum
WITH
customer_metrics AS (
    SELECT
        customer_id,
        COUNT(*) AS total_orders,
        SUM(total_amount) AS total_spent,
        AVG(total_amount) AS avg_order_value,
        MIN(order_date) AS first_order,
        MAX(order_date) AS last_order,
        CURRENT_DATE - MAX(order_date) AS days_since_last,
        MAX(order_date) - MIN(order_date) AS customer_lifespan_days
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY customer_id
),
customer_segments AS (
    SELECT
        *,
        NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY total_spent DESC) AS spending_quartile,
        NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY total_orders DESC) AS frequency_quartile,
        NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY days_since_last ASC) AS recency_quartile,
        -- CLV projeksiyonu (basit model: ortalama * beklenen siparis)
        CASE
            WHEN customer_lifespan_days > 0 THEN
                (total_orders::float / GREATEST(customer_lifespan_days, 1)) * 365
            ELSE 1
        END AS orders_per_year,
        avg_order_value * 3 * -- 3 yillik projeksiyon
        CASE
            WHEN customer_lifespan_days > 0 THEN
                (total_orders::float / GREATEST(customer_lifespan_days, 1)) * 365
            ELSE 1
        END AS projected_3yr_value
    FROM customer_metrics
)
SELECT
    CASE
        WHEN spending_quartile = 1 AND frequency_quartile = 1 THEN 'Sampiyonlar'
        WHEN spending_quartile <= 2 AND recency_quartile = 1 THEN 'Sadik'
        WHEN recency_quartile >= 3 AND spending_quartile <= 2 THEN 'Risk Altinda'
        WHEN recency_quartile = 4 THEN 'Kaybedilmis'
        ELSE 'Normal'
    END AS segment,
    COUNT(*) AS customer_count,
    ROUND(AVG(total_spent)::numeric, 2) AS avg_total_spent,
    ROUND(AVG(total_orders)::numeric, 1) AS avg_orders,
    ROUND(AVG(projected_3yr_value)::numeric, 2) AS avg_projected_clv
FROM customer_segments
GROUP BY 1
ORDER BY avg_total_spent DESC;

Alistirma 3.3: Recursive CTE - Organizasyon Hiyerarsisi (Organization Hierarchy)

Raporlama zincirlerini gosteren eksiksiz bir organizasyon agaci olusturun.

Recursive CTE Nedir?
Recursive CTE, hiyerarsik veya agac yapili verileri islemek icin kendini cagirir. Iki parcasi vardir: temel durum (baslangic noktasi) ve recursive durum (sonraki seviyeyi nasil bulacagi).

Gorev: Calisan-yonetici iliskisini dolasmak icin recursive CTE kullanin.

Adim Adim Yaklasim: Recursive CTE her zaman temel durum (anchor) ve kendine referans veren recursive durumdan olusur. Her parcayi olusturalim.
Adim 1: Temel Durum ile Basla (Kok Dugumler)

Soru: Organizasyon hiyerarsisinde "kok" calisanlar kimlerdir? Onlari nasil tanimlayabiliriz?

-- Temel durum: yoneticisi olmayan calisanlar (hiyerarsinin tepesi)
SELECT
    id,
    first_name || ' ' || last_name AS full_name,
    manager_id,
    1 AS level  -- Seviye 1'deler (en ust)
FROM employees
WHERE manager_id IS NULL;

Aciklama: WHERE manager_id IS NULL yoneticisi olmayan calisanlari bulur - genellikle CEO veya ust duzey yoneticiler.

Adim 2: Recursive Durumu Olustur

Soru: Daha once bulduklarimiza bagli olan calisanlari nasil buluruz?

-- Recursive durum: yoneticisi mevcut sonucumuzda olan calisanlari bul
SELECT
    e.id,
    e.first_name || ' ' || e.last_name,
    e.manager_id,
    oh.level + 1  -- Yoneticisinden bir seviye asagi
FROM employees e
JOIN org_hierarchy oh ON e.manager_id = oh.id;

Aciklama: Recursive kisim calisanlari mevcut hiyerarsiye (org_hierarchy) manager_id uzerinden baglar. Her iterasyon sonraki seviyeyi bulur.

Adim 3: Recursive'in Nasil Calistigini Anla

Soru: Veritabani recursive CTE'yi nasil calistirir?

Calistirma Akisi:

  1. Iterasyon 1: Temel durumu calistir ‚Üí CEO'yu bul (seviye 1)
  2. Iterasyon 2: Recursive durumu calistir ‚Üí CEO'ya rapor verenleri bul (seviye 2)
  3. Iterasyon 3: Recursive durumu tekrar calistir ‚Üí Seviye 2'ye rapor verenleri bul (seviye 3)
  4. Devam et satir donmeyene kadar

Her iterasyonun ciktisi, sonraki iterasyonun org_hierarchy referansi icin girdi olur.

Tam Cozum
WITH RECURSIVE org_hierarchy AS (
    -- Temel durum: ust duzey calisanlar (yoneticisi yok)
    SELECT
        id,
        first_name || ' ' || last_name AS full_name,
        manager_id,
        1 AS level,
        first_name || ' ' || last_name AS path,
        ARRAY[id] AS path_ids
    FROM employees
    WHERE manager_id IS NULL

    UNION ALL

    -- Recursive durum: yoneticisi olan calisanlar
    SELECT
        e.id,
        e.first_name || ' ' || e.last_name,
        e.manager_id,
        oh.level + 1,
        oh.path || ' > ' || e.first_name || ' ' || e.last_name,
        oh.path_ids || e.id
    FROM employees e
    JOIN org_hierarchy oh ON e.manager_id = oh.id
)
SELECT
    REPEAT('  ', level - 1) || full_name AS employee_tree,
    level,
    path,
    ARRAY_LENGTH(path_ids, 1) AS depth
FROM org_hierarchy
ORDER BY path;

Alistirma 4.1: Cizgi Grafikleri (Line Charts) icin Zaman Serileri (Time Series)

WITH
date_range AS (
    SELECT generate_series(
        (SELECT MIN(order_date) FROM orders),
        (SELECT MAX(order_date) FROM orders),
        '1 day'::interval
    )::date AS date
),
daily_sales AS (
    SELECT
        order_date,
        SUM(total_amount) AS sales,
        COUNT(*) AS order_count
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY order_date
)
SELECT
    dr.date,
    COALESCE(ds.sales, 0) AS sales,
    COALESCE(ds.order_count, 0) AS order_count,
    -- 7 gunluk hareketli ortalama
    ROUND(
        AVG(COALESCE(ds.sales, 0)) OVER (
            ORDER BY dr.date
            ROWS BETWEEN 6 PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW
        ),
        2
    ) AS moving_avg
FROM date_range dr
LEFT JOIN daily_sales ds ON dr.date = ds.order_date
ORDER BY dr.date;

Bolum 5: Zorluk Alistirmasi (Challenge Exercise)

Zorluk: Yonetici Panosu (Executive Dashboard) Sorgusu

Birden fazla analitik teknigini birlestiren kapsamli bir pano sorgusu olusturun.

Gereksinimler:

  • Mevcut ay ile onceki ay satis karsilastirmasi (Month-over-Month Comparison)
  • Gelire gore en iyi 5 urun (Top 5 Products by Revenue)
  • Musteri segment dagilimi (Customer Segment Distribution)
  • Hareketli ortalamali (Moving Average) gunluk satis trendi (Daily Sales Trend)
Ogrenme Yaklasimi: Bu karmasik bir pano sorgusudur. Bunu 4 bagimsiz parcaya bolelim - her biri kendi basina bir sorgu olabilir!
Adim 1: Aylik Satis Karsilastirmasi

Soru: Bu ayin satislarini gecen ayin satislariyla tek bir sorguda nasil karsilastirabiliriz?

-- CASE ile kosullu toplama kullan
SELECT
    SUM(CASE
        WHEN DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) = DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE)
        THEN total_amount ELSE 0 END) AS current_month,
    SUM(CASE
        WHEN DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) = DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '1 month')
        THEN total_amount ELSE 0 END) AS previous_month
FROM orders;

Aciklama: CASE ile kosullu SUM, veriler uzerinde tek geciste farkli kosullari toplamaya olanak tanir.

Adim 2: Gelire Gore En Iyi 5 Urun

Soru: Bu ay icin gelire gore en iyi 5 urunu nasil buluruz?

SELECT
    p.name,
    SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) AS revenue,
    ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) DESC) AS rank
FROM products p
JOIN order_lines ol ON p.id = ol.product_id
JOIN orders o ON ol.order_id = o.id
WHERE o.order_date >= DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE)
GROUP BY p.id, p.name;

Aciklama: ROW_NUMBER() urunleri gelire gore siralar. Sonra son sorguda rank <= 5 ile filtreleyebiliriz.

Adim 3: Musteri Segmentleri

Soru: Musterileri siparis sayilarina gore nasil segmentleriz?

SELECT
    CASE
        WHEN total_orders >= 10 THEN 'VIP'
        WHEN total_orders >= 5 THEN 'Normal'
        ELSE 'Ara Sira'
    END AS segment,
    COUNT(*) AS count
FROM (
    SELECT customer_id, COUNT(*) AS total_orders
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY customer_id
) cust
GROUP BY 1;

Aciklama: Ic sorgu musteri basina siparis sayar, dis sorgu onlari segmentlere kategorize eder.

Adim 4: Hareketli Ortalamali Gunluk Trend

Soru: 7 gunluk hareketli ortalama ile gunluk satislari nasil hesaplariz?

SELECT
    order_date,
    SUM(total_amount) AS daily_sales,
    ROUND(AVG(SUM(total_amount)) OVER (
        ORDER BY order_date
        ROWS 6 PRECEDING
    ), 2) AS ma_7d
FROM orders
WHERE order_date >= CURRENT_DATE - 30
GROUP BY order_date;

Aciklama: ROWS 6 PRECEDING 7 satirlik bir pencere olusturur (mevcut + onceki 6). Bu pencere uzerindeki AVG hareketli ortalamayi verir.

WITH
-- Aylik karsilastirma
monthly_comparison AS (
    SELECT
        SUM(CASE WHEN DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) = DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE)
            THEN total_amount ELSE 0 END) AS current_month,
        SUM(CASE WHEN DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date) = DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '1 month')
            THEN total_amount ELSE 0 END) AS previous_month
    FROM orders
),
-- En iyi urunler
top_products AS (
    SELECT
        p.name,
        SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) AS revenue,
        ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY SUM(ol.quantity * ol.unit_price) DESC) AS rank
    FROM products p
    JOIN order_lines ol ON p.id = ol.product_id
    JOIN orders o ON ol.order_id = o.id
    WHERE o.order_date >= DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE)
    GROUP BY p.id, p.name
),
-- Musteri segmentleri
customer_segments AS (
    SELECT
        CASE
            WHEN total_orders >= 10 THEN 'VIP'
            WHEN total_orders >= 5 THEN 'Normal'
            ELSE 'Ara Sira'
        END AS segment,
        COUNT(*) AS count
    FROM (
        SELECT customer_id, COUNT(*) AS total_orders
        FROM orders
        GROUP BY customer_id
    ) cust
    GROUP BY 1
),
-- Gunluk trend
daily_trend AS (
    SELECT
        order_date,
        SUM(total_amount) AS daily_sales,
        ROUND(AVG(SUM(total_amount)) OVER (ORDER BY order_date ROWS 6 PRECEDING), 2) AS ma_7d
    FROM orders
    WHERE order_date >= CURRENT_DATE - 30
    GROUP BY order_date
)
SELECT
    JSON_BUILD_OBJECT(
        'summary', (
            SELECT JSON_BUILD_OBJECT(
                'current_month_sales', current_month,
                'previous_month_sales', previous_month,
                'growth_pct', ROUND((current_month - previous_month) * 100.0 / NULLIF(previous_month, 0), 2)
            )
            FROM monthly_comparison
        ),
        'top_products', (SELECT JSON_AGG(JSON_BUILD_OBJECT('name', name, 'revenue', revenue)) FROM top_products WHERE rank <= 5),
        'segments', (SELECT JSON_AGG(JSON_BUILD_OBJECT('segment', segment, 'count', count)) FROM customer_segments),
        'daily_trend', (SELECT JSON_AGG(JSON_BUILD_OBJECT('date', order_date, 'sales', daily_sales, 'ma', ma_7d) ORDER BY order_date) FROM daily_trend)
    ) AS dashboard;

Onemli Cikarimlar (Key Takeaways)

Konu Ana Noktalar
Pencere Fonksiyonlari (Window Functions)
  • Toplamadan grup olusturmak icin PARTITION BY kullanin
  • LAG/LEAD karsilastirmalar icin komsu satirlara erisir
  • Cerceve ifadeleri hangi satirlarin dahil edilecegini kontrol eder
Ileri Toplamalar (Advanced Aggregations)
  • Birden fazla ozet seviyesi icin GROUPING SETS
  • Hiyerarsik alt toplamlar (hierarchical subtotals) icin ROLLUP
  • Kosullu toplama (conditional aggregation) icin FILTER
CTE'ler (Common Table Expressions)
  • Adim adim analitik icin CTE'leri zincirleyin
  • Hiyerarsik veriler icin recursive CTE'ler (recursive CTEs)
  • Karmasik sorgularin okunabilirligini artirin
Gorsellestirme Hazirligi (Visualization Preparation)
  • Tarih bosluklarini generate_series ile doldurun
  • API'ye hazir cikti icin JSON fonksiyonlarini kullanin
  • Gorsellestirme seviyesinde on-toplama (pre-aggregation) yapin
